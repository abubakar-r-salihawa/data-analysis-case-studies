# Car Price Analysis

This case study analyses 201 automobile records to identify the specifications most associated with price and to compare several regression models.

## Highlights

- Cleaned and validated the source data.
- Explored correlations between price and engine size, horsepower, curb weight and other specifications.
- Compared linear, ridge, polynomial and random-forest regression.
- Best test performance: **Random Forest, R² ≈ 0.936 and RMSE ≈ 2,799.90**.

Open `car_price_analysis.ipynb` for the complete workflow and conclusions.
